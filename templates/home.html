What is NestEgg?
NestEgg is a platform that allows incarcerated individuals to receive training and apply for jobs and housing in order to have a foothold before their release. By giving people a chance to prepare for life before leaving prison, our goal is to lower recidivism rates and turn incarceration into a restorative rather than punitive experience. By offering access to online trainings and certifications, classes in financial management and responsibility, pre-screened job applications, and affordable housing, it is our hope that people will leave the prison system in a better position to succeed than when they went in.
